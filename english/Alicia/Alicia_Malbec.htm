Our wines :: Viña Alicia :: Malbec
                
   

Malbec

Good alcohol. Intense violet. Good ageing.

Spicy, deep aromas. Fresh fruits, some of stones and minerals. Moka, figs, honney and cherries.

Location

 Luján de Cuyo county. 23 k. Southwest Mendoza City.

Altitude: 1200 m above sea level. "Viña Alicia" vineyard belongs to Mrs Alicia, and before was her father and grandfather´s property.

 Climate

 Cold autumns, very cold winters; rainless summers.

During summer, cold nights and warm days. Very good conditions for ripness.

Average summer temperatures: 21°C. Winter temperatures: -6°C. Annual rainfall 200-250 mm.

 Soil

 Loose, sandy and limy. Permeable. Stone and gravelly subsoil. In terraces 30 m above Mendoza River.

 Vineyard Description

 Low spallier. Guyot system. 5500 vines per ha.

15 hectars. 

Harvest 

Manually performed as from end of March and beginning of April. In 12-15 kg plastic cages.

 Stage in winery

 Cold maceration during 2 days. Then begins the alcoholic fermentacion:7-10 days. Natural yiests.

First fermentation: 25-28°C.

Malolactic fermentation: in barrels. Frequent recirculations during the first 4 days.

 Storage ageing time

 8 months, in new French oak barrels.

 Minimum bottle stowage 

10 months.

 Organoleptic characteristics

 Good alcohol. Intense violet. Good ageing.

Spicy, deep aromas. Fresh fruits, some of stones and minerals. Moka, figs, honney and cherries.
