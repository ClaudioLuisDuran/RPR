Nebbiolo

 

Location

Luján de Cuyo county. 18 km South Mendoza City.

Altitude: 900 m above sea level. San Alberto Vineyard. The property belongs to "Viña Alicia" since 1983.

Climate

Slow autumns, cold winters, very cold nights and warm days during summer. Ideal conditions for good ripeness and body.

Average summer temperatures: 25-23° C. Winter temperatures: -4-5°C. Annual rainfall 220 mm.

Soil

Alluvial origin. Clay and limestone. Permeable. Stone and gravelly subsoil.

Vineyard Description

10 has. Average of the vineyard: 20 years in an intensive density: 11.000  vines per ha.

Harvest

April. Manually performed in 12-15 Kg plastic cages.

Stage in winery

Short maceration: 7 days.

Fermentation temperature: 29°C. Recirculations: daily.

Storage ageing time

3 months in new American oak barrels, then 8 months in French barrels. Clarified with egg-whites. Botled without filtration.

Minimum bottle stowage

8 months.

Organoleptic characteristics

Intense ruby red, changing to garnet with time. Roses aromas, cassis, violets and a little of petrol.
