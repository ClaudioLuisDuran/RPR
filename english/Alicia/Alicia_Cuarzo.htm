Cuarzo

A blend of Petit Verdot, Carignan and Grenache Noir.

 Location

 Luján de Cuyo County. 18 km Southwest Mendoza City.

Altitude: 900 m above sea level. San Alberto Vineyard.

 Climate

 Slow autumns, cold winters, very cold nights and warm days during summer. The ideal conditions for good ripeness and full body.

Average summer temperature: 23°C. winter temperatures -4-5°C . Annual rainfall: 220 mm.

 Soil

 Alluvial origin. Clay  and limestone. Permeable. Stone and gravelly subsoil.

 Vineyard Description

 10 has. Average of the vineyard: 20 years in an intensive density: 11.000 vines per ha.

 Harvest

 April. Manually performed in 12-15 Kg plastic cages.

 Stage in winery

 The fermentation is done separatly.  Petit Verdot and Carignan: fermentation at 30-32°C. Grenache at 28°C.

Malolactic fermentation in French oak.

 Storage ageing time

 18 months in barrels.

Clarified with egg-whites. Bottled without filtration.

 Minimum Bottle stowage

 10 months.

 Organoleptic characteristics

 Intense garnet colour. Full bodied. Muscular. Granuled and enganging tannins. Earthy and truffled aromas. Hints of green pepper and ripe plums.
