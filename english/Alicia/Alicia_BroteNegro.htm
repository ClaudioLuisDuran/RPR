Brote Negro

Luján de Cuyo. 23 km  Southwest Mendoza City.

Altittude: 1200 m above sea level. "Viña Alicia" vineyard.

 Climate

 Cold autumns, very cold winters, rainless summers.

During summer, cold nights and warm days. Very good conditions for ripness. Average summer temperatures: 21°C. winter temperatures -6. Annual rainfall 200-250 mm.

 Soil

 Loose, sandy and limy. Permeable. Stone and gravelly subsoil. In terraces 30 m above Mendoza River.

Vineyard Description 

Low spallier. Guyot system. 5500 vines per ha.

15 hectars.

 Harvest

 Manually performed as from end of March. In 12-15 kg. plastic cages.

 Stage in winery

 Fermentation: 10-12  days. Temperature of fermentation: 28-30°C.

Frecuent recirculations: twice a day.

 Storage ageing time

 12 months, 50% new French oak and 50% one year French oak barrels.

 Minimum bottle stowage

 10 months.

 Organoleptic characteristics

 Intense purple color. Fresh fruits an regaliz, Damacena plum, violets, strawberries and a touch of vanilla, spices and raisins.
