Chardonay

Soft yellow with greenish lightings.

To the nose, we find notes of banana, apple, pear, melon, honey and some notes of biscuit.

Greasy palate, good acidity. We also find citric tastes with peaches and pineapple.  The end is exceptionally harmonic and balanced
