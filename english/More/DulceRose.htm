Anastasia Dulce Rosé

Anastasia Rosé - Sparkling Wine

Blend made of: Ugni Blanc - Chenin Blanc - Malbec

Lugar: Luján de Cuyo (Perdriel), Mendoza, at 980 meters above sea level.

Present a beautiful salmon color, with persistent and fine
bubbles. In nose is very fruity. In mouth is sweet, with a balanced acidity and right volume. Must be served cool at 6 or 8ºC
