Merlot

Technical notes: 2004 harvest
. 100% Merlot.
. Selected low production of grapes and water management, gives us excellent fruit concentration.
. 20% of sangria, with 7 days of pre-fermentation. Maceration in cold temperatures.
. Slow fermentation process in Taransaud 55hl. French oak casks.
. Controlled malolactic fermentation after the alcoholic one, grants the softness needed in mouth.
. Hand picked harvest in 18 kgs baskets and double manual selection of the grapes and bunches.   

Tasting notes
“The ample temperatures gap between day and night helps our vineyards to find very deep colours. Intense fruit, cherry, eucalyptus and mint flavour emerge to combine with the excellent wood notes and grant a great combination. Strong structure and long mouthfeel are responsible for this very unique wine.

Statistic information
. Appeal (Region): Alto Agrelo, Lujan de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina.
. Altitude: 980 mts above sea level.
. Composition: 100% merlot.
. Vineyard: sections 5 and 6 east of Pulenta Estate -16 year old vineyard.
. Production per hectare:  60 to 80 hl
. Ageing: 12 months in 100% new french oak barrels.
. Alcohol: 14%
. Acidity: 6 g/l total (Tartaric Acid)
. Ph: 3.65
. Bottled: August 2005
. Production: 14.970 bottles.
