Malbec

Technical notes: 2004 harvest
. 100% Malbec.
. The concentration of the fruit is the result of good pruning and drip irrigation.
. Long maceration in Taransaud 55hl casks gives this wine a strong personality from the very beginning.
. Controlled malolactic fermentation to assure the acidity softness in mouth.
. Hand picked harvest in 18 kgs. baskets and double manual selection of the grapes and bunches.  

Tasting notes
“The ample temperature gap between day and night allows good ripeness of bunches and intense fruit character, where the plum, cherry, vanilla, and the sweetness of the wood are notoriously present. The well-finished tannins, the deep intense colour, and the long persistency in mouth are the pillars of this wine's personality."

Statistic information
. Appeal (Region): Alto Agrelo, Lujan de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina.
. Altitude: 980mts above sea level.
. Composition: 100% Malbec.
. Vineyard: vineyard: section 6 west of Pulenta Estate -16 year old vineyard.
. Production per hectare:  60 to 70 hl
. Ageing: 12 months in 100% new french oak barrels.
. Alcohol: 14%
. Acidity: 5.92 g/l total (Tartaric Acid)
. Ph: 3.75
. Bottled: October 2005
. Production: 28.182 bottles.
