Our wines :: Pulenta Estate
                
 

Pulenta Estate is the winery and vineyards from the brothers Hugo and Eduardo Pulenta. Is located in Alto Agrelo, Lujan de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina. 135 hectares of 16 years old high quality vineyards have excellent climate conditions. Irrigation comes from melted water from the Andes Mountains. Altitude reaches 980m over sea level.
