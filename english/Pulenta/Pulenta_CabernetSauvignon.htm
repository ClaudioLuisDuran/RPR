Cabernet Sauvignon

Technical notes: 2004 harvest
. 100% Cabernet Sauvignon.
. Selected low production of grapes to obtain ripe characters and intense colours.
. Long maceration in Taransaud 55hl french oak casks allows us to obtain excellent soft structured tannins in wines.
. 100% malolactic fermentation. This process is constantly under supervision of the winemaker to obtain a balanced acidity.
. Hand picked harvest in 18 kgs. baskets and double manual selection of the grapes and bunches.

Tasting notes
“Its ruby colour, red berry marmalade, red pepper, and a final dash of oak gives this wine complexity and interesting aroma. The intense character and excellent finish of this wine is the result of the ripe tannins obtained from the Alto Agrelo Cabernet Sauvignon"
   
Statistic information
. Appeal (Region): Alto Agrelo, Lujan de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina.
. Altitude: 980 mts above sea level.
. Composition: 100% cabernet sauvignon.
. Vineyard: sections 9 and 10 east of Pulenta Estate - 16 year old vineyard.
. Production per hectare:  60 to 70 hl
. Ageing: 12 months in 100% new french oak barrels.
. Alcohol: 14%
. Acidity: 6 g/l total (Tartaric Acid)
. Ph: 3.68
. Bottled: August 2005
. Production: 14.404 bottles.
