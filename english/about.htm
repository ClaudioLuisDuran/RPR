About our mission
                
RPR Importers LLC was created out of an idea and an opportunity to provide to the consumer market in the United States and other select countries around the World, unique wines and other agricultural products from Argentina.

For example, some of the best wines of Argentina are hidden in small wineries with a tradition of excellence, but without the marketing resources to export and distribute outside Argentina. We have combined the knowledge of the local grape growing and wine making industry and culture with the knowledge of the US market to take advantage of niche opportunities and bring such products to the consumer market.

RPR Importers has the local knowledge and resources in Argentina with partners on the field that identify agricultural products, arrange for packaging, exporting and importing to meet our customers requirements. We have the flexibility to adapt and work in any kind of relationship that best meets the needs of our customers. Our services include searching for unique products, arranging for customized packaging, developing private label brands, exporting, importing, brokering and distribution.

For customers who want to market their own private label products or want market some unique products on an exclusive basis, we offer to find these products of choice, taking advantage of our experienced presence in Argentina, conduct tests to confirm the required product characteristics, develop private label packaging, obtain regulatory approvals, and then package, ship, import, store and deliver to the customer’s destinations(s). Throughout the whole process, we monitor every step to ensure that the products qualities are preserved.
